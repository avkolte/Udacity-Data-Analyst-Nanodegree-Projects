Relations between Prosper Loan Data¶

by Anup Kolte¶

Investigation Overview¶

The observation is on original loan amount, borrower's Prosper rating, loan term, borrower's stated monthly income. The factors influence Prosper rating.

Dataset Overview¶

Dataset has 81 attributes and it is consisted of borrower APRs and attributes of 113,937 loans. My main focus is on original loan amount, borrower's Prosper rating, loan term, borrower's stated monthly income these attributes.

Distribution of Borrower APR¶

The distribution of APR looks multimodal. A small peak centered at 0.1, a large peak centered at 0.2. There is also a small peak centered 0.3. Additionally, there is a very shape peak between 0.35 and 0.36. Only very few loans have APR greater than 0.43.

Prosper Rating Occurences¶

ProsperRating is an ordinal categorical variable with the values from higher to lower ('AA', 'A', 'B', 'C', 'D', 'E', 'HR'), ratings in data set centered at 'C' the highest 'AA' and the lowest 'HR' ratings are least.
